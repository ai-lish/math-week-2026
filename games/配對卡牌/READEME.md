## 📐 Ch12 全等三角形（OLE 延伸）

### 全等條件配對（5對）

| 對 | 代數面 A（條件） | 圖像面 B（示意） |
|----|----------------|----------------|
| 1 | **SAS** | 兩邊及其夾角相等（標示兩邊夾角） |
| 2 | **ASA** | 兩角及其夾邊相等（標示兩角夾邊） |
| 3 | **AAS** | 兩角及另一邊相等（標示兩角一邊，非夾邊） |
| 4 | **SSS** | 三邊全部相等（標示三邊） |
| 5 | **RHS** | 直角、斜邊及另一邊相等（直角三角形，標示斜邊） |

### 圖像提示詞（MiniMax Image-01）

```
Two congruent triangles ABC and DEF drawn side by side, 
SAS condition highlighted with red marks on two sides and included angle,
labels "AB=DE", "BC=EF", "∠B=∠E", clean vector style, white background
```

→ 5張圖，分別對應 SAS / ASA / AAS / SSS / RHS

## 🖼️ MiniMax 圖像生成提示詞

### 全等三角形（Ch12）

**SAS:**
```
Two congruent triangles ABC and DEF drawn side by side on white background,
SAS condition highlighted with red arcs and tick marks: AB=DE, BC=EF, angle B=angle E,
labels show equal sides and angle, clean math diagram vector style
```

**ASA:**
```
Two congruent triangles ABC and DEF drawn side by side on white background,
ASA condition highlighted: angle A=angle D, angle B=angle E, side AB=DE,
tick marks on equal sides, arcs on equal angles, clean math diagram style
```

**AAS:**
```
Two congruent triangles ABC and DEF drawn side by side on white background,
AAS condition highlighted: angle A=angle D, angle B=angle E, side BC=EF (not between equal angles),
tick marks and arc notation, clean math diagram style
```

**SSS:**
```
Two congruent triangles ABC and DEF drawn side by side on white background,
SSS condition highlighted: all three sides marked with tick marks AB=DE, BC=EF, CA=FD,
all sides labeled equal, clean math diagram style
```

**RHS:**
```
Two congruent right triangles ABC and DEF drawn side by side on white background,
RHS condition highlighted: right angle at C and F marked with squares, hypotenuse AB=DE marked with double ticks, side BC=EF marked with single tick,
clean math diagram style
```

### 平行線角（Ch11）

**同位角:**
```
Two parallel horizontal lines cut by a transversal slanting line,
corresponding angles marked with same color arcs on top-left of intersection,
labels "a" and "a" showing they are equal, clean geometry diagram style white background
```

**內錯角:**
```
Two parallel horizontal lines cut by a transversal slanting line,
alternate interior angles marked with same color arcs between the parallels,
labels "b" and "b" showing they are equal, clean geometry diagram style white background
```
